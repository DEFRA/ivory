Help our experts by allowing us to share details of your item

To further their knowledge and understanding of pre-1918 ivory items of outstandingly high artistic, cultural, and historical value, we'd like to share details of your application with a closed group of 'Prescribed Institutions'. Prescribed Institutions (opens in a new window or tab) are the experts who will be assessing the ivory items.

We will only share:

  • information about the item from your application, such as the description
  • photographs of the item declared in your application
  • the outcome of your application
  • this information once you have received the outcome of your application

No personal data will be shared.
